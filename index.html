FAQ

What is Meltdown?
Meltdown is Phoenix's premier 24-hour hackathon where students come together to build amazing projects.
Who can participate?
Anyone under 18 with a passion for technology and innovation! Whether you're a complete beginner with no experience or a skilled expert, there's room for you!
What should I bring?
Bring your laptop, charger, a water bottle, and a positive mindset. We'll provide food, drinks, and a comfortable space to work.
Is it free?
Yes! The event is completely free to attend.
